Let's A Fun Game

Rules of the game

  • The game has 2 players, playing in turn

  • In each turn, a player can either rolls a dice as many times as he/she wishes or pass the turn to next player. After rolled dice, each getting rolled result get added to his Total score

  • But, if the player rolls a 1, The player his total score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

  • The next player also can choose to either rolls a dice as many times as he/she wishes or pass the turn to next player.

  • The first player to reach 100 points on Total score wins the game
