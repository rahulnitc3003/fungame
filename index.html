Let's A Fun Game

Rules of the game

  • The game has 2 players, playing in turn

  • In each turn, a player either rolls a dice as many times as he/she whishes or pass the turn to next player. Each result get added to his/her current score

  • Player saves the current score into it's final score in each turn with press of hold score

  • But, if the player rolls a 1, The player his/her current score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

  • The next player also can choose to either rolls a dice as many times as he/she wishes or pass the turn to next player.

  • The first player to reach 100 points on final score wins the game
